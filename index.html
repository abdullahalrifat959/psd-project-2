Who are we as a Clinic?

B&C Health Kids is a new and exciting division of B&C Health, an established clinic that offers comprehensive assessment, treatment and consultation services. We foster a culture of excellence with a focus on ethical and evidence-based practice that meets the needs of the very diverse community we serve. We offer comprehensive assessment, treatment and consultation services for children and adolescents. Our standards are high, and the work is challenging, but always rewarding and fulfilling. As community psychologists our mission is to help our clients make substantial and lasting changes in their lives. We are located near the Village of Streetsville in Mississauga right off of Highway 401.

Our Mission & Values

B&C Health (bnchealth.ca) is a well established and growing psychologyclinic that offers comprehensive assessment, treatment, and consultation services. Ourteam of registered psychologists is committed to providing evidence­based services in aprofessional and respectful environment. We employ Cognitive­Behavioural Therapy(CBT) and related techniques for a variety of difficulties from depression and anxiety tothe stress and strain of work and everyday life. We are located 30 minutes west ofToronto in Mississauga.

Benefits of working at B&C

Prime Location: We are located 30 minutes west of Toronto in Mississauga with easy access to major highways and public transportation.

Flexible working hours: Our clinic is staffed with administrative support from 8 am to 8 pm ensuring coverage is available for all schedules.

Diverse Client Base: Our team prides ourselves on our relationships with insurance companies, family practices and other referral sources offering our clinicians a variety of assessment and treatment opportunities.
